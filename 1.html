HTML이란 무엇인가?

HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is the standard markup language used for creating and designing documents on the World Wide Web.

It structures web pages by defining elements like headings, paragraphs, links, images, and other content, and is interpreted by web browsers to display content to users. HTML allows for the embedding of multimedia, linking to other web pages, and defining the layout and design of a webpage.
